Preston on Wheels was built on a simple idea: great auto repair shouldn't mean a tow truck, a waiting room, and a dealer-sized bill.

Preston is a full-service mobile mechanic serving the Cape Fear region. He brings the shop to your driveway: the tools, the parts, and years of hands-on experience, and treats every vehicle like it's his own.

Customers consistently mention the same things: he shows up on time, he's meticulous about cleaning and greasing every part, he hauls off the old parts, and he leaves the job site spotless. And because there's no storefront overhead, the price is honest, often about half of a dealer or independent shop.
