Good Questions

Frequently asked

Do you come to me?

Yes, that's the whole idea. Preston comes to your home, workplace, or roadside with all the tools and parts needed for the job. No tow truck, no sitting in a waiting room.

What areas do you serve?

We cover the Cape Fear region: Wilmington, Leland, and Burgaw, North Carolina, plus the surrounding communities. If you're close by and not certain you're in range, give us a call, we travel.

What kinds of repairs can you do on-site?

Most everything a traditional shop handles: oil changes, brakes, batteries, spark plugs, starters & alternators, struts & shocks, ball joints, water pumps & radiators, tire rotation, and full engine diagnostics. Don't see your repair listed? Just ask.

How much will it cost?

You get a fair, honest quote up front, with no surprises. Because there's no shop overhead, jobs often run about half of what a dealer or independent shop would charge.

How do I book an appointment?

Call or text Preston at 910-756-7261, or email clymerautoservice@gmail.com. Tell us your vehicle, the issue, and where you're located, and we'll get you scheduled.

Do I need to supply the parts?

No. Preston brings the parts and tools needed and disposes of the old parts properly, leaving your space clean. If you'd prefer to supply your own parts, that's fine too. Just let us know.
